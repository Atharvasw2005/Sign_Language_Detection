About Us

Understanding Sign Language Detection

Our Sign Language Detection application bridges communication gaps between the deaf community and those unfamiliar with sign language. Using advanced AI and computer vision, we've created a tool that:

  • Recognizes Indian Sign Language (ISL) alphabets and numbers
  • Detects common phrases and expressions
  • Converts sign language to text in real-time
  • Utilizes text-to-speech for audible communication

With MediaPipe and TensorFlow at its core, our application uses sophisticated machine learning models trained on thousands of sign language gestures. The technology tracks hand landmarks in 3D space and analyzes movement patterns to accurately interpret signs.

We're committed to making communication more accessible and inclusive. Our application continues to learn and improve, expanding its sign language vocabulary and detection accuracy with each update.
